ClubHub Account Deletion

Last updated: April 8, 2026

If you want your ClubHub account deleted, you can request deletion using the contact method below.

How to Request Deletion

  • Send an email with subject: Account Deletion Request.
  • Include your username and the club name linked to your account.
  • We may request confirmation to protect your account from unauthorized deletion.

Deletion Request Email: your-email@example.com

App Name: ClubHub

What Gets Deleted

  • Your login account and direct profile access.
  • Associated personal account metadata required for authentication.

What May Be Retained

  • Records required for legal, security, audit, or financial compliance purposes.
  • Operational backups for a limited retention period.
